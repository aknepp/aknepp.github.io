Internship - CEM

The Purdue University Construction Engineering Management department needed a new interface to manage student data regarding their internships that were completed through the program.

I created a new system with C# and SQL to accommodate Advisors and Students through different user permissions. The students are able to access their own personal page where they can update their personal data and view recommended companies to apply to based on their location and industry preferences.

The advisors serve as admins for the website. They can add and delete both students and companies as they enter and leave the CEM program. The advisors have full access to analyze the student and company data. An extra and interesting feature that was requested is that the students are able to write a review on their previous employer and the advisors could see the reviews on the company information pages.

Javascript, HTML, C#, SQL, Bootstrap
